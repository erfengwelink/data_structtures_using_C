# Stack

A stack supports 3 basic operations:
1> push

    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     
    | 6 | 3 |   |---->| 9 | 2 |   |---->| 7 | 1 |   |---->NULL
    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     

                                 |
                                \|/

    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+
    | 6 | 3 |   |---->| 9 | 2 |   |---->| 7 | 1 |   |---->| 1 | 0 |   |---->NULL
    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+

2> pop

    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+
    | 6 | 3 |   |---->| 9 | 2 |   |---->| 7 | 1 |   |---->| 1 | 0 |   |---->NULL
    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+

                                 |
                                \|/

    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     
    | 6 | 3 |   |---->| 9 | 2 |   |---->| 7 | 1 |   |---->NULL
    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     



3> peek

    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+
    | 6 | 3 |   |---->| 9 | 2 |   |---->| 7 | 1 |   |---->| 1 | 0 |   |---->NULL
    +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+     +-----------+
                                                                /|\
                                                                 |
                                                                 |
                                                               (TOP)
